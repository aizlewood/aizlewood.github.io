What is this thing?

With so many UX-related events around, it felt time to put some focus back on visual design... or UI design? Digital design? Web design? Whatever you want to call it, there's a lot there and a lot to talk about. This local, monthly and free meetup will let designers show their work, share any methods, processes and tools, and ask for the odd critique.

Any questions? Tweet @aizlewood.

FAQ

  • What is it?

    F&F is a Brighton-based monthly meet-up for digital designers to meet, critique, learn and discuss all things related to visual, UI, app and graphic design.

  • Any speakers?

    F&F looks to expose interesting speakers and discussions revolving around the visual design discipline.

  • How is it structured?

    2x talk at Middle Street, followed by beer and pizza for chatting, discussions and networking

  • Who runs it?

    F&F is MC'd by yours truly. I'll be introducing speakers, finding new speakers and curating topics and subject matter.

  • I'm interested in speaking. What do I do?

    F&F is a free event, so speakers are volunteering their time to practice their talks, extend their message, or just rant about something they want off their chest.

  • Why does this thing exist?

    Design feels underserved in the community. There are a tonne of UX events, and dev have their fair share. Oddly, visual design feels left out. F&F wants to help change that.

  • Sounds good. What next?

    Stay tuned to the twitter account, website and your inbox for news and updates. We'll be announcing our first event soon.
